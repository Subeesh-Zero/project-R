About Result Portal

This portal allows students to check their academic results online. Please enter your roll number, department, and date of birth to access your result.

For any issues, please contact your department office.

Privacy Policy
Data Collection

We only collect the information necessary to retrieve your academic results, including your roll number, department, and date of birth.

Data Usage

Your information is used solely for the purpose of fetching and displaying your academic results. We do not store any personal data on our servers.

Data Security

All data transmissions are encrypted using HTTPS protocol to ensure your information remains secure.
